About Morris Motors

Who am i?

I'm Jamie Morris, and have been a mechanic for over a decade; in both work time and personal time, i live and breathe cars; I've worked on everything from Vauxhalls, to Audis, to Mazeratis. I've done anything from your basic wheel replacement, up to turbo diagnosics and engine rebuilds. I now work mobile, giving you the flexability to go about your day without having to interupt your day by heading down the local garage.

Quotations

For quotations, email or call me (the details are below), in most instances i can quote or give estimates over the phone. If you can reference any error codes, it'll help me give you quick and accurate numbers.

Where can you Service?

I'm Luton based; and can generally service as far as Milton Keynes, St.Albans, Hatfield, and surrounding areas. For larger Jobs i can travel further; please drop me a message, or call, to find out what i can do for you.

Why Should you Try a Mobile Mechanic?

It makes your life easier. You know where your car is; you know it's being worked on (and not just sitting in the shop); and you can go about the rest of your day. no need to get lifts back from the local garage, or time your day around it. I can come to you, work on it at your's.

Can i see your recommendations?

Of course! Morris motors is proudly open on facebook so you can see what other people think. Our social media links are on the "contact" page
